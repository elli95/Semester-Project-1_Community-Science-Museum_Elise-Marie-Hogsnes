Visit us

Location

The museum is located at Kjelsåsveien 143, 0491 Oslo.

Opening hours

Monday-Friday: 10:00 – 16:00

Saturday: 9:00 – 16:00

Sunday: Closed

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

You can order your tickets in the form below, payment takes place on arrival at the museum. If you would like to organize a trip for 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the trip.
